Entities Badges Test mode

Carnation

Rushes through rooms and kills anyone in sight. Makes a pink effect appear near the edges of your screen when active. Hide in a hiding spot or a saferoom to survive

Heed and Slight

Slight appears in a room with blue glow. Dont look at it. If you get hit by it twice in one run - you die Heed is the opposite, look at it

Goatman

Sets a timer between saferooms. Appears when time runs out and starts rapidly approaching from a random side of the map. Flash to push it back

Slugfish

Makes a similar effect to Carnation but its white. It sweeps the rooms. Move out of its way

Elkman

Appears near the exit to a room and starts making a static effect on the player. Move behind any obstacle to survive and make it go away. You may go through it as it has no collision.

Dozer

Appears along with carnation. You must sit still when it appears. Gives about 1 second before attacking

Sorrow

Makes the rooms look red and shakes the screen. Go under something like a doorframe or a brick wall to survive.
